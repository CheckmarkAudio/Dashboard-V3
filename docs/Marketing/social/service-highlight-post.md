# Service Highlight Post

## Purpose

Highlight a service in a reusable way that can be adapted for different audiences and channels.

## Use When

- Promoting a core service.
- Reintroducing an existing offer.
- Supporting a campaign with simple awareness copy.

## Do Not Use When

- The post needs a detailed case study.
- The service details are still changing.

## Template

[Audience] often need [Outcome] without [Common Friction].

That is where [Service Name] helps.

It gives you [Primary Benefit], while keeping [Secondary Benefit] simple and manageable.

[Primary CTA]

## Customization Notes

- Replace `[Audience]` with a specific group.
- Keep `[Common Friction]` honest and recognizable.
- Use a CTA that matches the channel, such as "Send us a message", "Book a call", or "Learn more".

## Review Checklist

- Audience is specific.
- Service name is current.
- Benefits are accurate.
- CTA points to the correct next step.
