# Craigslist Vocal Recording Post

## Purpose

Promote vocal recording services for singers, rappers, voiceover artists, songwriters, and creators.

## Use When

- Advertising studio vocal recording sessions.
- Promoting local recording availability.
- Filling open studio time.

## Do Not Use When

- Studio availability, rates, or engineer coverage are not confirmed.
- The service includes claims that have not been approved.

## Template

**Post Title:** Record Vocals That Sound Clean, Confident, and Ready to Share - [City/Area]

**Category:** Services / Creative Services

**Location:** [City/Neighborhood]

Got a song, demo, voiceover, or creative project that needs vocals to sound clear and confident?

[Studio Name] offers vocal recording sessions in [City/Area] for artists and creators who want a comfortable studio environment, a focused recording workflow, and someone helping keep the session moving.

Whether you are tracking a hook, a full song, a reference vocal, a voiceover, or content audio, the goal is to help you get solid takes without the session feeling rushed or intimidating.

Great fit for:

- Singers recording demos or release-ready vocals
- Rappers tracking verses, hooks, and ad libs
- Songwriters capturing polished references
- Voiceover, podcast, and content recording
- Artists who need help getting confident takes

Sessions can include:

- Vocal tracking
- Basic comping and take organization
- Engineer support during the session
- Playback review
- File delivery after the session

**Session length:** [Minimum session length]

**Rate:** [Rate or starting price]

**Availability:** [Days or general availability]

**Location:** [Studio area or neighborhood]

Reply with:

- What you are recording
- Preferred date/time
- How many songs or scripts you want to track
- Any reference tracks or notes

Send the basics and we will respond with available times and the best next step for your session.

## Customization Notes

- Confirm whether mixing, tuning, or editing are included before posting.
- Keep the rate and minimum session length current.
- Add booking links only when approved.
- Do not promise release-ready results unless the package includes full production, mixing, and mastering.

## Review Checklist

- Rate and session length are accurate.
- Included services are clear.
- Studio location is safe and approved for public posting.
- Reply instructions match the booking workflow.
