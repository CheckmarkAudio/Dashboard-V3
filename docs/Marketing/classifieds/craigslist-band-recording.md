# Craigslist Band Recording Post

## Purpose

Promote recording services for bands, ensembles, and full-group music projects.

## Use When

- Advertising local band recording sessions.
- Promoting studio availability for demos, singles, EPs, or live-style recordings.
- Reaching musicians who are comparing studio options.

## Do Not Use When

- Drum tracking, room availability, or engineer staffing are not confirmed.
- The studio cannot support the instrumentation described in the post.

## Template

**Post Title:** Bring the Band In and Capture the Sound You Have Been Building - [City/Area]

**Category:** Services / Creative Services

**Location:** [City/Neighborhood]

Your band has the songs. Now you need a recording space that helps you capture the energy without making the process complicated.

[Studio Name] offers recording sessions for bands and music groups in [City/Area]. Whether you are working on a demo, single, EP, live-style session, or pre-production tracks, we can help you walk in with a plan and leave with recordings you are excited to share.

Great fit for:

- Rock, indie, worship, punk, acoustic, pop, and songwriter projects
- Bands preparing a demo or release
- Groups that need structured studio time
- Artists who want help planning the recording workflow

Recording options can include:

- Full-band tracking
- Instrument overdubs
- Vocal tracking
- Guitar, bass, keys, and percussion recording
- Session planning and file delivery

**Session length:** [Minimum session length]

**Rate:** [Rate or starting price]

**Availability:** [Days or general availability]

**Studio notes:** [Room details, drum tracking availability, gear notes, or limitations]

Reply with:

- Band name
- Genre
- Number of songs
- Instruments you need to record
- Preferred dates
- Whether you need live tracking, overdubs, or both

Send the details and we will follow up with available times and recommended session options.

## Customization Notes

- Confirm whether drums can be tracked before posting.
- Keep gear and room details accurate.
- Clarify whether mixing and mastering are separate services.
- Use genre examples that match the studio's real strengths.

## Review Checklist

- Instrument support is accurate.
- Rate and session minimum are current.
- Studio limitations are clear.
- Reply instructions collect enough information for booking.
