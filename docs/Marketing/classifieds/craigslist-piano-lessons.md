# Craigslist Piano Lessons Post

## Purpose

Promote local piano lessons through Craigslist or another classified-ad channel.

## Use When

- Advertising private piano lessons.
- Filling available weekly lesson slots.
- Promoting beginner, intermediate, or customized music instruction.

## Do Not Use When

- The schedule, rate, or lesson location is not confirmed.
- The teacher bio or lesson format still needs approval.

## Template

**Post Title:** Piano Lessons That Make Music Feel Fun and Doable - [City/Area]

**Category:** Services / Lessons & Tutoring

**Location:** [City/Neighborhood]

Want piano lessons that feel encouraging, practical, and actually fun to come back to each week?

[Instructor or Studio Name] offers private piano lessons for [kids/adults/beginners/intermediate students] in [City/Area]. Lessons are built around real progress without making music feel stiff or overwhelming.

Students can work on reading music, chords, technique, favorite songs, ear training, songwriting basics, or performance prep. The goal is simple: help each student feel more confident at the piano and excited to keep playing.

Lessons are a good fit for:

- New students starting from scratch
- Returning players who want structure again
- Kids building confidence at the piano
- Adults who want a practical, enjoyable way to learn
- Students who want help with songs, theory, or performance

**Lesson format:** [In-person / online / both]

**Lesson length:** [30 / 45 / 60 minutes]

**Rate:** [Rate or starting price]

**Availability:** [Days or general availability]

To ask about openings, reply with:

- Student age or experience level
- Preferred lesson day/time
- Main goal for lessons

Send a quick message and we can find the right lesson fit.

## Customization Notes

- Add the correct city or neighborhood in the title and location line.
- Keep the rate current.
- Mention the studio or instructor name only if approved for public posting.
- Add a portfolio, website, or booking link only when Craigslist allows it and the link is current.

## Review Checklist

- City and lesson format are accurate.
- Rate and availability are current.
- Reply instructions are clear.
- No private contact details are included unless approved.
