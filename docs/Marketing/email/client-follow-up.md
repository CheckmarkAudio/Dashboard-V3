# Client Follow-Up Email

## Purpose

Follow up with a client after a conversation, booking, campaign touchpoint, or completed service.

## Use When

- A client needs a next step.
- A recent interaction should be summarized.
- Marketing needs a reusable follow-up message that feels personal but structured.

## Do Not Use When

- The client needs a legal, billing, or support-specific response.
- The next step is unclear.

## Template

**Subject:** Next steps for [Project or Service]

**Preview text:** A quick recap and the next action.

Hi [First Name],

Thanks for taking the time to connect about [Project or Service].

Based on what we discussed, the next best step is [Next Step]. This will help us [Client Benefit].

You can [Primary CTA] here: [Link]

Thanks,

[Sender Name]

## Customization Notes

- Make the first sentence specific to the recent interaction.
- Keep the benefit focused on the client outcome.
- Replace `[Primary CTA]` with a direct action such as "book a time", "review the draft", or "confirm the details".

## Review Checklist

- The next step is clear.
- The link is correct.
- The tone matches the client relationship.
- All placeholders have been replaced.
