# Email Template Standard

## Purpose

Use this file as the base structure for any reusable marketing email.

## Use When

- Creating a new client, prospect, partner, or internal marketing email.
- Turning a successful one-off email into a repeatable template.
- Preparing email copy for future portal access.

## Do Not Use When

- The message is a private client-specific note that should not be reused.
- The email depends on an expired promotion, event, or deadline.

## Template

**Subject:** [Specific subject line]

**Preview text:** [Short context that supports the subject]

Hi [First Name],

[Opening line that states why this message matters to the reader.]

[One short paragraph explaining the offer, update, resource, or reason for reaching out.]

[One short paragraph with the benefit or next step.]

[Primary CTA]

Thanks,

[Sender Name]

## Customization Notes

- Replace every bracketed placeholder.
- Keep the CTA to one action.
- Adjust tone based on the relationship with the recipient.
- Confirm any offer, deadline, price, or claim before sending.

## Review Checklist

- Subject line is clear.
- Preview text is not a duplicate of the subject.
- Audience and CTA match.
- No private or stale details remain.
- All placeholders have been replaced.
