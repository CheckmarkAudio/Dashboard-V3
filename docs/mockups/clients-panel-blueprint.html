Placement rules
  • `Add Client` stays top-right and primary.
  • `Edit` becomes a direct row action instead of living only in the 3-dot menu.
  • `Book` belongs on the client row so a client can flow directly into booking.
  • `Archive` can stay behind `More` for active clients.
  • `Restore` should be visible for archived clients.
